How to use this layout planner

  1. Add stories — paste titles one per line in Manual entry and click Add stories, or switch to Import CSV and click to browse for your Google Sheets export.
  2. Set type & pages — each story gets two dropdowns: type (Feature, Boilerplate, etc.) and page count. Stories tagged as Front cover, Back cover, IFC, or IBC are automatically pulled into the cover spreads at the top.
  3. Build layout — the top two spreads show your four covers. Interior pages flow continuously below as a light table, 1–2 side by side, then 3–4, and so on.
  4. Drag to reorder — grab the ⠿ handle on any interior story and drag it up or down. All its pages move together and page numbers update automatically.
  5. Export — Export PDF opens your browser's print dialog; choose Save as PDF. Export text downloads a plain-text runsheet.
Color code: blue = Feature · green = Boilerplate · orange = Ad · red = Front/Cover story · purple = IFC/IBC · gray = Back cover. The counter in the toolbar shows pages used vs. remaining.
